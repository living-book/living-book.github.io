- `biophilia` (nature-fix) — Biophilia: Humans carry an evolved, largely innate affinity for living systems and natural settings, not merely a learned aesthetic preference.
- `nature-dose-response` (nature-fix) — Nature's Dose-Response Curve: Nature's benefits scale with duration and intensity of exposure — minutes lower stress, hours restore mood, but multi-day immersion crosses a further cognitive threshold.

- `myelin-insulation` (talent-code) — Myelin Insulation: Skill is physically the growth of myelin, an insulating sheath that wraps repeatedly-fired neural circuits, making signals faster and more accurate.
- `talent-hotbeds` (talent-code) — Talent Hotbeds: Small, humble, resource-poor places sometimes produce a wildly disproportionate share of world-class performers when deep practice, ignition, and coaching converge there.
- `negative-visualization` (the-antidote) — Negative Visualization (Premeditatio Malorum): The Stoic practice of vividly imagining a feared loss in advance, which shrinks anxiety by replacing an unbounded fear with a bounded, survivable picture.
- `group-synchrony` (when-perfect-timing) — Group Synchrony (Boss, Tribe, Heart): Groups that must move in time together need three distinct ingredients — an external pacesetter, a felt sense of belonging, and a shared purpose — and synchronizing itself boosts well-being.
