# Study Guide: Chapter 11 — The Multiverse

## Core Idea
Taken literally as explanation, quantum theory describes a multiverse of parallel, differentiating histories. No collapse needed: measurement is ordinary differentiation, probability is proportions of fungible instances. The best explanation, believed rather than instrumentalized.

## Key Terms
Multiverse · fungibility · differentiation/interference · measure problem · Born rule

## Case Summary
Single-photon interference: fungible instances in briefly-diverged histories recombining — fringes no single-history story explains without magic.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Find a model you use daily while refusing its implications
- [ ] Try explanatory literalism: believe the best model, follow what follows
- [ ] Track differentiation events, not labels, when reasoning about copies
