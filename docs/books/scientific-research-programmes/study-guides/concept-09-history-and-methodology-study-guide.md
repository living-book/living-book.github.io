# Study Guide: Concept 9 — History and Methodology

## Core Concept
Methodologies are research programmes; history is their test. Meta-criterion: the best methodology reconstructs the most great science as rational (internal history), leaving the least residue to sociology (external history).

## Key Terms
Internal/external history · meta-criterion · rational reconstruction · footnote discipline

## Case Summary
Lakatos's provocation: rational reconstruction in the text, actual deviations in the footnotes. The caricature measures how much of the record reason can claim.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Test your decision framework against your three best past calls
- [ ] Keep footnotes: where wins deviated from the official method
- [ ] Measure the framework's residue honestly — size of the footnotes
