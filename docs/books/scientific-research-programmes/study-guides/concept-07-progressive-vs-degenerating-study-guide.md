# Study Guide: Concept 7 — Progressive vs Degenerating

## Core Concept
The demarcation criterion: progressive programmes predict novel facts (some corroborated); degenerating ones only accommodate after the fact. Science/pseudoscience is a property of trajectory, not content — and verdicts are provisional (programmes recover).

## Key Terms
Progressive/degenerating problemshift · novel fact · use-novelty · accommodation

## Case Summary
Einstein: light-bending predicted before Eddington measured — stunning novelty. Marxism, per Lakatos's audit: failed predictions patched by excuses predicting nothing.
