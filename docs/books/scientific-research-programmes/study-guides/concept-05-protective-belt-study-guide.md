# Study Guide: Concept 5 — The Protective Belt

## Core Concept
Auxiliaries, instruments, initial conditions — the adjustable periphery takes every hit by design. Anomalies become routed work items. Adjustments must be content-increasing (predict new facts), not ad hoc (explain backwards only).

## Key Terms
Protective belt · ad hoc (1/2/3) · content-increasing shift · blame routing

## Case Summary
Neptune: belt adjustment predicting a findable planet — glory. Vulcan: same maneuver, predicting nothing findable, repeated — decay.

- [ ] Grade your last fix: predicted improvement or backward re-description?
