# Study Guide: Concept 8 — No Instant Rationality

## Core Concept
Appraisal ≠ advice. The score is objective but announced in retrospect; staying with a degenerating programme or gambling on an immature one can both be rational. The only sin: denying the public record.

## Key Terms
Instant rationality (myth) · hindsight crowning · rational disagreement · honest scorekeeping

## Case Summary
Michelson–Morley 1887: decades as a mere anomaly, crowned "crucial" only after Einstein's programme progressed. The caption came a quarter-century after the experiment.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Reclassify one real-time "verdict" you've issued as the bet it was
- [ ] State aloud which of your positions is a defended gamble, with its score
- [ ] Define what honest score would flip your biggest professional bet
