# Study Guide: Concept 6 — The Positive Heuristic

## Core Concept
The programme's battle plan: a pre-scheduled sequence of increasingly realistic models. Anomalies of crude stages are foreseen and queued — scientists rationally ignore most refutations because the plan already books them.

## Key Terms
Positive heuristic · model sequence · relative autonomy of theory · planned idealization-lifting

## Case Summary
Newton's curriculum: point-sun → multiple planets → extended bodies → spinning spheroids → tides. Each stage known-false in advance; each failure scheduled.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Draft your model sequence: which assumption does each version lift?
- [ ] List anomalies the plan already schedules — stop reacting to them
- [ ] Date the last problem your plan (vs your bug tracker) generated

## Self-Test
1. Why aren't programme scientists paralyzed by anomalies?
2. Roadmap vs backlog in Lakatosian terms?
3. What happens when the heuristic runs out?
