# Study Guide: Concept 2 — Sophisticated Falsificationism

## Core Concept
Theories are falsified only by better theories: rivals that predict novel facts, retain the old successes, and get some novelty corroborated. Evidence alone never executes; competition does. Crucial experiments are hindsight honors.

## Key Terms
Duhem problem · novel fact · crucial experiment · theory succession

## Case Summary
Uranus's anomalies → auxiliary planet → Neptune found (triumph). Mercury's anomalies → "Vulcan" → never found; Newton outlived it anyway until Einstein offered the better digestion.
