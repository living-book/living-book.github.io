# Study Guide: Chapter 13 — Progress through Revolutions

## Core Concept
Science progresses from primitive beginnings, not toward a goal — Darwin applied to knowledge. Puzzle-solving power grows undeniably; "closer to truth" does no explanatory work. Progress is real, directional, and goalless.

## Key Terms
Progress-from · evolutionary analogy · instrumental progress · no-miracles argument (rival)

## Case Summary
Kuhn's design spec for a progressive field: shared exemplars, peer-only judgment, insulation from lay demands, education-driven turnover — community structure, not method, explains success.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Reframe one goal from "find the right X" to "increase solving capacity"
- [ ] Identify which of your fitnesses is adapted to a shifting environment
- [ ] Strip a hidden "destination" from your team's definition of progress
