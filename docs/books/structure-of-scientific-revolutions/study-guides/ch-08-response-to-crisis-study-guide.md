# Study Guide: Chapter 8 — The Response to Crisis

## Core Concept
Anomalies never refute alone: paradigms are abandoned only when a rival exists. Theory choice is triadic — paradigm vs paradigm vs nature. Crisis behavior: redoubled effort, defections, philosophical debate, wild speculation from the least-invested.

## Key Terms
Paradigm comparison · rule-loosening · extraordinary science · rejection-with-replacement

## Case Summary
Generations rationally patched Ptolemy; Copernicus went to first principles. Einstein: "the ground pulled out from under one" — physics fled to philosophy, then retooled.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Convert one complaint ("X is broken") into a comparison ("Y solves what breaks")
- [ ] Build the alternative before campaigning against the incumbent
- [ ] Source wild options from the least legacy-invested people
