# Study Guide: Chapter 5 — The Priority of Paradigms

## Core Concept
Paradigms are deeper than rules: communities cohere on shared concrete exemplars even when they can't agree on definitions. Knowledge transmits through worked examples and trained similarity-perception.

## Key Terms
Exemplar · family resemblance · tacit knowledge · similarity relations

## Case Summary
Physics education: force is learned through the inclined plane and pendulum, not through definitions. Practitioners hesitate on rules, converge on problems.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Curate three gold-standard exemplars for your team's craft
- [ ] Test: same definitions, divergent cases? Your practice is fragmenting
- [ ] Rewrite one rules-document as a worked-examples document
