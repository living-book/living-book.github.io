# Study Guide: Chapter 10 — Revolutions as Changes of World View

## Core Concept
Paradigm change restructures perception itself — a gestalt switch with no neutral data underneath. Scientists don't reinterpret fixed observations; trained categories determine what is seen.

## Key Terms
Gestalt switch · theory-laden observation · duck-rabbit · world change

## Case Summary
The swinging stone: Aristotle sees constrained fall, Galileo sees a pendulum — different phenomena, different measurables, different science. Post-Copernican astronomers promptly "see" novelties in an unchanged sky.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] List what your training makes you see instantly — then what it hides
- [ ] Staff your next review with differently-trained eyes (different objects, not opinions)
- [ ] After a framework switch, budget for perceptual retraining, not just learning
