# Study Guide: Chapter 3 — The Nature of Normal Science

## Core Concept
Normal science actualizes the paradigm's promise: determining significant facts, matching facts to theory, articulating the theory. It doesn't seek novelty — restriction of vision is precisely what enables depth and precision.

## Key Terms
Mop-up work · articulation · fact-theory match · three problem classes

## Case Summary
A century of Euler, Lagrange, and Laplace completing Newton: making the Moon's orbit obey the *Principia*. Not testing Newton — fulfilling him.

## Application Checklist
- [ ] Sort your backlog: extension-of-framework vs challenge-to-framework
- [ ] Identify the unglamorous precision work your field's progress depends on
- [ ] Stop demanding disruption where refinement is the need
